Meet Your Coach

Certified coach. Genuine passion for the game.

Golf taught me patience, focus, and the quiet satisfaction of getting better at something. My mission is to give every school student in Auckland that same experience.

As a fully qualified NZGTF Golf Teaching Professional, I coach in a way that meets kids where they are — no experience required, no pressure, just great fundamentals delivered in a way that's genuinely fun. In 2026 I became the Official Golf Coach at Auckland University, working with diverse groups that range from complete beginners to confident players.

GoGolf exists because I believe every school student deserves access to the game. I bring the equipment, the structure, and the energy — your school just needs to say yes.
