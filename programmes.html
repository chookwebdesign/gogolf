Weekly School Sessions

Once a week, Sam comes to your school with all the gear needed for a complete 60-minute golf session. No travel, no logistics, no stress for staff — just show up and play.

Groups of 10–20 students

No prior experience needed

FAQ

Common questions

Everything you need to know before booking. Don't see your question? Just ask us.

Our school programmes are designed primarily for primary and intermediate school students (ages 5–13). Sessions are skill-level based, not age-based, so mixed-ability groups work well. Contact us to discuss your specific group.

Light rain isn't necessarily a problem — real golfers play in all conditions! For heavier rain or unsafe weather, we'll work with your school to reschedule or move the session to an indoor space if available (a hall works well for putting and short games). We'll always communicate in advance if a session needs to change.

No. Sessions are designed to run on your school's existing outdoor space — a field, court, or open grassed area is perfect. We set up temporary targets and equipment as part of our setup. If you have an indoor hall, that can work for certain activities too.

Pricing is per child. Schools can be invoiced directly, or parents can pay individually — whichever is easier for your administration. We're flexible. Get in touch and we'll work out the simplest arrangement for your school.
